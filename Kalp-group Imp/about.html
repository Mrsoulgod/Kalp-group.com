Our History

B2B Specialty Chemicals with Global Standards

Kalp Consumer Products began operations with a singular focus: to deliver raw material solutions of uncompromised purity to industrial manufacturers. Over the years, we have transitioned from a localized trader to an integrated supplier of high-purity oleochemicals, industrial green solvents (including FAME bio-solvents derived from soya, rice bran, mustard, palm, and castor oil), and advanced polymer additives.

We maintain robust relationships with major primary manufacturers and biorefineries, allowing us to safeguard supply lines from standard market volatility. Today, procurement officers and formulation chemists count on us for stable lead times, technical alignment, and strict adherence to specification tolerances.

Headquartered in New Delhi, India, we manage multi-location warehouse nodes and logistics channels, enabling rapid regional dispatch and streamlined custom clearances for our global export lines.

Quality Check & Infrastructure

Advanced Quality Verification Controls

Every single batch we trade or manufacture undergoes a multi-point verification protocol before loading. Our in-house analytical lab validates core physical-chemical indexes such as acid value, iodine value, hydroxyl numbers, color index, and flashpoints.

By integrating rigorous verification systems with batch-traceable documentation, we help customers mitigate compounding risks and avoid costly assembly stop-times. We offer comprehensive Certificate of Analysis (COA) records and technical support files with every order.

Technical Documentation

We supply full batch records, certificate of analysis (COA) records, and technical datasheets (TDS) with every commercial delivery.

Foundation & Vision 2017

Kalp Consumer Products was founded with a clear vision to revolutionize the specialty chemicals and nutraceuticals supply chain. We laid down our primary core values, focused on sourcing high-purity, bio-based raw materials and building robust logistic channels across regional industrial hubs.

Launch of Edible Oils 2019

Expanding our market presence, we launched our Edible Oils division, integrating state-of-the-art refining processes to manufacture premium food-grade and therapeutic oils. This phase significantly expanded our agricultural sourcing network and consumer product footprint.

Global Oleochemical Expansion 2021

In 2021, we scaled our manufacturing capacity and initiated our global oleochemical expansion. By introducing high-purity dimer acids, fatty acids, and vegetable-sourced glycerine to international markets, we successfully positioned ourselves as a key partner for global industrial manufacturers.

Corporate Responsibility

Green Chemistry & Ethical Trade

As a supplier of oleochemicals, we recognize that sustainable sourcing is not just a trend but an operational imperative. We actively prioritize suppliers who source oilseeds through certified deforestation-free networks.

Our product selection highlights bio-based alternatives that reduce carbon footprints across their life-cycles. By trading materials that degrade safely back into the ecosystem without sacrificing performance, we support the sustainability goals of our corporate buyers.

Furthermore, we ensure ethical labor conditions are upheld across our supply partners, confirming that every drum of material delivered complies with fundamental human welfare agreements.
