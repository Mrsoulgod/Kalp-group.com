Sustainable Bio-Based Green Solvents and FAME Mediums for Industrial Compounding

Kalp Consumer Products manufactures and distributes eco-friendly Bio-Based Solvents & Fatty Acid Methyl Esters (FAME) alongside traditional hydrocarbons, polar oxygenated chemicals, and chlorinated agents. We ensure sustainable and reliable supply pipelines for formulation chemists and industrial manufacturers.

We maintain rigorous checks on parameters such as isomer stability, flashpoint ranges, evaporation rates, and trace moisture contents, assuring process consistency across your compounding runs.

FAME of Soya Oil

FAME of Soya Oil

Purpose of use & Application

  • Highly effective bio-solvent serving as a sustainable alternative to chlorinated hydrocarbons and mineral spirits.
  • Exceptional solvency for printing inks, alkyd paints, and organic coating formulations.
  • Provides a low-volatility carrier medium for crop protection and agricultural sprays.

Company's Positioning

  • Kalp supplies Soya Methyl Ester (SME) with high active ester content (>98%) and low moisture index.
  • Maintains bulk reserves to secure stable supply timelines for paint and coatings manufacturers.
Application: Paints, coatings, printing inks, and agricultural adjuvants.
FAME of Rice Bran Oil

FAME of Rice Bran Oil

Purpose of use & Application

  • Sustainable bio-solvent offering excellent viscosity control and metal-wetting characteristics.
  • Widely used in industrial degreasing, emulsifiers, and metal cleaning compound formulations.
  • Serves as a non-toxic carrier oil for veterinary pharmaceuticals and agricultural adjuvants.

Company's Positioning

  • Sourced from premium local rice bran oil refining lines, ensuring high stability and consistency.
  • Custom-packaged in standard steel drums or bulk ISO-containers to fit client plant setups.
Application: Industrial cleaning, metal degreasing, and agrochemical formulations.
FAME of Mustard Oil

FAME of Mustard Oil

Purpose of use & Application

  • Bio-solvent displaying a very low pour point and excellent low-temperature flow characteristics.
  • Utilized as an additive in bio-lubricants, metalworking fluids, and industrial release agents.
  • Acts as a high-flashpoint, stable dissolving medium for tough industrial resins.

Company's Positioning

  • We control the transesterification process closely to minimize free glycerol contents and preserve low viscosity.
  • Guarantees strict compliance with international B2B purity standards for lubricants.
Application: Bio-lubricants, mold release agents, and metalworking fluids.
FAME of Palm Oil

FAME of Palm Oil

Purpose of use & Application

  • High thermal stability bio-solvent widely utilized in oil-field chemicals, mud formulations, and mining processes.
  • Acts as a clean, eco-friendly fuel blending component and industrial heater solvent.
  • Functions as a processing aid and viscosity reducer in rubber compounding.

Company's Positioning

  • We offer Palm Methyl Ester (PME) derived from sustainably managed plantations, fully certified for corporate export chains.
  • Sourced to ensure exact cetane index values and low cloud points for stable performance.
Application: Oilfield mud chemicals, fuel blending, and rubber processing.
FAME of Castor Oil

FAME of Castor Oil

Purpose of use & Application

  • Unique ricinoleic-based methyl ester providing superior lubricity, high polarity, and plasticizing properties.
  • Excellent carrier for high-performance coatings, polyurethanes, and specialized varnishes.
  • Acts as a highly compatible organic plasticizer in elastomer and biopolymer compounds.

Company's Positioning

  • Kalp is a leading manufacturer of premium castor derivatives. Our Castor Methyl Ester features low color value and high purity.
  • Developed with technical precision to ensure exact hydroxyl numbers for downstream polyurethane formulations.
Application: Polyurethane resins, specialty varnishes, bio-plasticizers, and precision machinery lubricants.
