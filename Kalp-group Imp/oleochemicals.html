Industrial Oleochemicals

Natural fatty intermediates derived from renewable oils and fats

Kalp Consumer Products is a supplier of high-purity fatty acids and bio-based compounds. We deliver industrial oleochemical lines designed to meet the technical specifications of formulating chemists and production managers globally.

Our catalog focuses on chemical stability, consistent carbon-chain length control, and secure batch traceabilities across high-volume shipments.

Fatty Amines

Fatty Amines

Purpose of use & Application

  • Cationic surfactant intermediates utilized as industrial adhesion promoters, corrosion inhibitors, and flotation collectors.
  • Performs as active anti-stripping agents in bitumen formulations for road construction.
  • Acts as dispersing and emulsifying agents in agricultural adjuvant products.

Company's Positioning

  • Kalp supplies high-purity primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines refined from natural seed oils.
  • Sourced to ensure stable amine values and low moisture indices for downstream ethoxylation runs.
Application: Bitumen emulsions, agrochemicals, and water treatment.
Fatty Alcohols

Fatty Alcohols

Purpose of use & Application

  • Fatty alcohols are linear primary alcohols functioning as viscosity regulators, emollients, and co-emulsifiers.
  • Provides structurizing benefits in cosmetics, cream bases, and personal care formulations.
  • Feedstock intermediate for the manufacturing of alcohol ether sulfates (SLES) and industrial ethoxylates.

Company's Positioning

  • Sourced with precise carbon chain distributions (C16 Cetyl, C18 Stearyl, and C16-C18 Cetostearyl blends) meeting B2B standards.
  • Highly consistent, low-odor fatty alcohols with excellent heat stability.
Application: Cosmetics, detergents, personal care products, and pulp defoamers.
Fatty Aldehyde

Fatty Aldehyde

Purpose of use & Application

  • Aliphatic aldehydes featuring highly specific carbonyl reactivities and aroma characters.
  • Crucial synthesis intermediates for aroma chemicals, specialty flavor molecules, and fine perfumery compounding.
  • Functions as organic accelerators and cross-linking catalysts in specialty rubber formulations.

Company's Positioning

  • Our fatty aldehydes are stored and packaged under solid nitrogen blankets to prevent spontaneous conversion to fatty acids.
  • Sourced with reactive purity indexes above 98% to ensure minimal formulation trace impurities.
Application: Aromatics compounding, flavor synthesis, and polymer cross-linking.
Hydrogenated Castor Oil Flakes

Hydrogenated Castor Oil Flakes

Purpose of use & Application

  • Hard, high-melting vegetable wax used as an efficient rheological control agent, thickener, and anti-settling additive.
  • Enhances performance of industrial coatings, sealants, heavy-duty greases, and cosmetics.
  • Provides excellent resistance to water, oils, and solvents in specialty polymer formulations.

Company's Positioning

  • We manufacture premium castor derivatives with strict control over acid value and melting point (85-88°C).
  • Supplied in clean, dust-free uniform flakes for seamless blending and process reliability.
Application: Paint rheology, lubricating greases, cosmetics, hot-melt adhesives, and plastics.
Industrial Green Solvents

Industrial Green Solvents

Purpose of use & Application

  • Eco-friendly, bio-degradable solvents derived from natural vegetable oils, replacing traditional hydrocarbon solvents.
  • Excellent solvency for coatings, printing inks, agricultural adjuvants, and industrial cleaning formulations.
  • Non-toxic, low-VOC profiles to satisfy occupational environmental directives and support green initiatives.

Company's Positioning

  • Kalp supplies high-purity Fatty Acid Methyl Esters (FAME) custom-refined from soya, rice bran, mustard, palm, and castor oil.
  • Maintains stable bulk inventory and coordinates direct supply chains to guarantee consistent specifications.
Application: Paints, coatings, printing inks, agricultural formulations, and eco-friendly cleaning.

Polymer Additives

Polymer Additives

Purpose of use & Application

  • Functional compounding chemicals that prevent thermal, oxidative, or solar degradation during molding.
  • Includes PVC heat stabilizers, slip agents (Erucamide/Oleamide), organic plasticizers, and primary/secondary anti-oxidants.
  • Improves melt flow properties and controls cycle durations during high-shear injection or film extrusion.

Company's Positioning

  • We specialize in supplying RoHS-compliant, lead-free complex stabilizers and non-migrating bio-plasticizers.
  • Delivers custom pelletized or granular stabilizer packs that ease compounding hopper additions.
Application: PVC pipes, wire insulation, polymer masterbatches, and packaging films.
