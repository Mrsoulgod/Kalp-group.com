Performance Modifiers

Functional additives to optimize resin compounding and durability

Kalp Consumer Products supplies a functional catalog of PVC heat stabilizers, B2B processing aids, organic plasticizers, polymer anti-oxidants, and slip modifiers. We support plastic converters and masterbatch compounders with reliable raw materials.

Our product ranges are selected to minimize plate-out issues, prevent polymer degradation under extrusion shear, and secure color stability across various application conditions.

Heat Stabilizers

Heat Stabilizers

Purpose of use & Application

  • Scavenges released hydrochloric acid (HCl) during thermal extrusion processes, preventing polymer cracking and degradation.
  • Controls polymer coloration and prevents darkening under continuous heat shear forces.
  • Stabilizes electrical properties in flexible wire insulation compounds.

Company's Positioning

  • We specialize in Calcium-Zinc and Barium-Zinc non-toxic stabilizer blends, replacing legacy lead compounding lines.
  • Formulated to suppress baseline color shifts while maintaining polymer heat-aging parameters.
Application: PVC piping, electrical wire coatings, window frames, and flexible profiles.
Plasticizers & Processing Aids

Plasticizers & Processing Aids

Purpose of use & Application

  • Lowers resin glass transition temperatures, enhancing elasticity and stretch characteristics of rigid polymers.
  • Regulates resin fusion behavior during moulding to secure smooth surface finishes and minimize melt fracture defects.
  • Improves melt flow index (MFI) values to optimize productivity speeds.

Company's Positioning

  • Sourced to deliver low volatility and high compatibility matrices, preventing plasticizer migration or surface sweating.
  • We specialize in phthalate-free alternatives (such as DOTP and Epoxidized Soybean Oil - ESO) for sensitive B2B markets.
Application: Vinyl footwear, medical compound tubes, custom sheets, and synthetic leathers.
Anti-Oxidant Additives

Anti-Oxidant Additives

Purpose of use & Application

  • Suppresses degradation cycles caused by oxygen exposure during extrusion compounding and product service life.
  • Neutralizes alkoxy and alkyl free radicals to prevent structural chain scission or resin cross-linking failures.
  • Prevents polymer yellowing and maintains physical elasticity under long-term environmental stress.

Company's Positioning

  • Kalp trades synergistic blend packages containing both primary phenolic and secondary phosphite anti-oxidants in low-dust dustless granular forms.
  • High thermal stability grades suited for engineering resins (Nylons, Polycarbonates).
Application: PP/PE film packaging, hot melt adhesive systems, and injection molded auto parts.
Slip & Anti-Block Agents

Slip & Anti-Block Agents

Purpose of use & Application

  • Migrates to cooled film surfaces to form a thin lubricating layer, lowering coefficient of friction (COF).
  • Minimizes adhesion forces between adjacent film layers to prevent "blocking" or sheet fusion under storage pressures.
  • Enhances release characteristics for closure caps and mold releases.

Company's Positioning

  • Sourced with exceptionally low acid index thresholds (under 0.2) to eliminate odour or discoloration on finished packaging.
  • Offers excellent dispersion properties that avoid printability or laminating failures.
Application: Polyethylene films, slide closures, high-speed wrapping operations, and yarn spinning.
